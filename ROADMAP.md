# Future Feature Roadmap (Brainstorming)

## 1. "Ask Gita" AI Assistant (Semantic Chat)
- **Concept:** Conversational interface for life questions ("I feel anxious", "How to focus?").
- **Implementation:** Use the existing vector search backend. 
- **Value:** Makes wisdom instantly applicable personalized.

## 2. "Share the Wisdom" (Image Creator)
- **Concept:** Generate shareable cards for Instagram/WhatsApp.
- **Features:** Select Shloka -> Choose Background (Sunsets, Krishna) -> Custom Font -> Share.
- **Value:** Viral marketing loops; users love sharing spiritual quotes.

## 3. Guided Reading Plans (Sadhana)
- **Concept:** Pre-set schedules (e.g., "Gita in 30 Days", "7 Days of Karma Yoga").
- **Features:** Progress tracking, daily reminders.
- **Value:** Enhances retention and habit formation.

## 4. Personal Notes & Highlights ("My Gita")
- **Concept:** Allow highlighting text and adding personal reflection notes.
- **Value:** Transforms app from "reader" to "study companion".

## 5. Chanting Trainer (Karaoke Mode)
- **Concept:** Word-by-word highlighting sync with audio.
- **Value:** Assists with correct Sanskrit pronunciation.

## 6. Gamification
- **Concept:** Reading streaks, "Chapter Completed" badges.
- **Value:** Improves daily engagement.

## 7. Sanskrit Learning Tools
- **Concept:** Tap-to-translate individual words (Anvay expansion).
- **Value:** Scholarly depth.
